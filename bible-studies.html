Ladies’ evening Bible study

Meeting in the home of one of the leaders, this is an event for all ladies where you will find a warm welcome and an informal atmosphere; with Bible study, discussion... and tea or coffee! We meet from 7:45pm until 9:00pm.

Young women’s Bible study

We meet for coffee at 9:45am. The study is from 10:15am to 11:15am. This is an opportunity to meet together in the home of one of the leaders in a welcoming and relaxed atmosphere.

Bible study and prayer meeting

This is - essentially - the 'power-house' of the church, when, after our Bible study, we spend time in corporate prayer focussing on the many aspects of church life.

Fellowship Groups replace this meeting on the second Wednesday of each month, when we meet in homes around the Upton and Chester area for a time of more interactive Bible study, discussion and prayer.
