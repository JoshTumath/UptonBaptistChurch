Art group

Thursday

10:00am Art Group

Runs from 10:00am to 12:00pm in the church building. UBC Art Group offers you the opportunity to try your hand at painting. Whether you are an 'I would love to try' person or a more experienced painter, there is something for you. Tuition is also given if required.

The session starts with tea coffee and a chat then the 'thought for the day'.
