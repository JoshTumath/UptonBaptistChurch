We would love to be able to welcome you to our worship services. [summarise what happens in the services]

Communion is held every week, alternating between the morning and evening services. This is where we have bread and wine together to remember the death and resurrection of our Lord Jesus Christ.

An induction loop system is installed for the benefit of hearing aid users.

Morning worship service

In the morning, we have a family friendly service followed by refreshments.

Everyone gathers together at the start of the service. After the children are called to the front for a talk, they leave for their classes upstairs.

A crèche, with video link, is also available during the morning service.
