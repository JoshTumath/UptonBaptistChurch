Tiger Tots

Our Toddlers group meets during term-time on Monday mornings from 9:30am to 11:30am. Everyone is welcome – mums, dads, grandparents, carers...

We have a large, comfortable room where the children can play with a variety of toys including the weekly craft activity. We also have a safe baby area for new born and very young babies – a good place to meet other new parents. The session ends with a story and songs. Before the summer break we have a mum’s meal out in an evening, and there is a children’s party at Christmas time.

Please note that, due to the numbers on the waiting list, we have had to close this for the time being.

Tiny Tigers

Every Wednesday from 1.30 to 3.30, a group for new mums and babies aged 0 to 12 months. Come and meet other new parents. £1 per session.
