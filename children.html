Tuesday Club

For school years 3 to 6. We meet 6:15pm to 7:30pm in church each Tuesday evening during term time for a lively programme which is a mix of various games and craft work followed by Bible stories told in an interesting way.

Come and join with us, make new friends (and meet old ones).

Friday Club

We meet on Friday evenings from 7:30pm to 9:00pm (sometimes finishing later when trips out are organized and in the summer term). Alternate Fridays include chill out games – table tennis, pool, badminton, organized games and activities and a gospel talk. There is also a tuck shop.

On the other Fridays we have a Bible study at the home of one of the leaders where we investigate what a particular book of the Bible has to say to teenagers today, or we may address one of the big issues of the day.

Friday Club is a great way to make new friends and learn about God.
